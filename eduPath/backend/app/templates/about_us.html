Our Mission and Story

Edupath is dedicated to empowering baccalaureate students in making well-informed academic and career decisions. Our mission is to provide students with the tools and resources they need to find the best major and university that suits them using AI technology. We believe that every student deserves the opportunity to pursue their passions and achieve their goals. Our story began with a vision to revolutionize the way students navigate their educational journey, and we continue to strive towards that goal every day.

Discover Your Perfect Major and University with QuizMatcher

QuizMatcher is an innovative tool that utilizes AI to help students find the best majors and universities that suit their interests and goals. By taking a personalized quiz, students can uncover their strengths, passions, and career aspirations, allowing them to make well-informed academic and career decisions. With QuizMatcher, finding the perfect fit has never been easier.
