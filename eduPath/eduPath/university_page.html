National Higher School of Artificial Intelligence

Overview

Description

The National School of Artificial Intelligence is an institution of excellence for education whose vocation is to educate engineers specializing in the theory of artificial intelligence and data sciences. They will also have the capacity to develop and deploy practical and innovative solutions to the problems of the various socio-economic sectors (health, energy, agriculture, transport, etc.), thus contributing to the scientific and economic development of Algeria.
